<p>The Multipaz team would like to announce the release of Multipaz 0.94.0! Some highlights include:</p>
<p>&#8211; Improved and standards-compliant DCQL support in OpenID4VP<br />
&#8211; Support for EU Age Verification ISO mdoc document type<br />
&#8211; Native support for W3 Digital Credentials API on Android<br />
&#8211; OpenID4VCI support with ProvisioningModel and ProvisioningClient<br />
&#8211; Much more!</p>
<p><a class="ArnTpmDpqOjShEweVaJcYZwkyLmDqkqjbDdY " href="http://verifier.multipaz.org/" target="_self">verifier.multipaz.org</a>, <a class="ArnTpmDpqOjShEweVaJcYZwkyLmDqkqjbDdY " href="http://csa.multipaz.org/" target="_self">csa.multipaz.org</a>, <a class="ArnTpmDpqOjShEweVaJcYZwkyLmDqkqjbDdY " href="http://issuer.multipaz.org/" target="_self">issuer.multipaz.org</a>, and the backend for the Multipaz Identity Reader have also been updated. Prebuilt APKs for our Test App and Identity Reader are available to download from <a class="ArnTpmDpqOjShEweVaJcYZwkyLmDqkqjbDdY " href="https://apps.multipaz.org/" target="_self">https://apps.multipaz.org</a>. To learn more about Multipaz or contribute, visit <a class="ArnTpmDpqOjShEweVaJcYZwkyLmDqkqjbDdY " href="http://multipaz.org/" target="_self">multipaz.org</a>.</p>
